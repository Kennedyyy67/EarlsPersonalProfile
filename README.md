# EarlsPersonalProfile


I developed this personal profile to serve as a centralized digital space for my student journey and interests. The application provides a comprehensive look at my motivations for pursuing my studies, my various hobbies, and my contact information. To enhance user engagement, I integrated a custom audio feature that allows visitors to interact with a soundboard of my pet.


Features:
-Interactive Audio Interface: A custom-built soundboard designed to play curated audio clips of my pet.

-Student Profile: A narrative section detailing my educational background and the core reasons behind my academic path.

-Interests and Hobbies: A structured display of my personal pursuits and the activities that inspire my work outside the classroom.
